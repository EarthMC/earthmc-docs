# SMPEarth
## Introduction
Spmearth was a private Minecraft earth server based on [EarthMC](https://earthmc.net). It was played by many large Minecraft youtubers, most notably [Technoblade](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nh39Ap6lx9k) and [Wilbur Soot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N9Qcp1xUqAg&list=PL6WHJUJ5a3FlvMmXK1pw7aXYTQlF6Jrnt). It was a huge success and their videos attracted millions of views. 

## What happened to SMPearth?
The server was shut down 11-04-2020 after being online for 142 days.

## SMPEarth map download
The map has been uploaded to the internet archive and can be downloaded from [here](https://archive.org/download/smp-earth-map). After downloading the zip file, extract the world folder in to \Users\...\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft\saves. This will allow you to play the world locally on singleplayer and explore the SMPEarth map.

## SMPEarth alternative
SMPEarth opened in 2019 and was based on [EarthMC](https://earthmc.net). EarthMC is public and has been online since 2016. You can join it with the ip address **play.earthmc.net**. Further join instructions can be found [here](https://earthmc.net/docs/earthmc-ip-address).
